Once your Tradier API is connected, BiggsTrade checks your open trades every minute during market hours. When a trade hits its take profit, stop loss, expiry, or time exit — a banner appears at the top of the app telling you exactly what to do. You never have to watch a chart — BiggsTrade watches for you.
